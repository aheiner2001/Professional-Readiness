In addition to any other questions they would like to ask, please have your friend ask at least a few from this list of common questions:
Tell me about yourself?
What is one of your strengths?
What is one of your weaknesses?
Tell me about a time when you had to overcome a difficult technical problem.
Tell me about a time when you had to overcome a disagreement or conflict with other people.
Tell me about a project you have worked on. What was one of the most difficult parts of the project? Why did you choose to do it the way you did?
(After any other response) Can you give me a specific example of a time when you have done that?
At the end of each interview, you should ask your friend or family member the following question:
What do you think I could do better next time?
